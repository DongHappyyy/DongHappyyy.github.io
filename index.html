About Me

I am currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree at the School of Artificial Intelligence and Automation, Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) , under the supervision of Prof. Luxin Yan and Prof. Yi Chang. Since 2025, I have also been participating in a joint Ph.D. training program at Hanjiang Laboratory.

I received the B.E. degree and M.S. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Qingdao University, Qingdao, China, in 2020 and 2023, respectively. During my Master's study, I was supervised by Prof. Guodong Wang.

Interests

My research interests focus on event-based vision, particularly object tracking and object detection using event cameras.

Key Words: Event Camera, Visual Object Tracking, Object Detection.
